About the Workshop

This workshop focuses on evaluation, monitoring, and regulatory challenges of AI-enabled medical systems. It aims to connect AI researchers, clinicians, and regulatory scientists.

  • Monitoring and drift detection
  • Regulatory science for AI
  • Clinical deployment challenges
